COMPANY PROFILE

Lazcom Solar and Electrical Services Limited is a Company registered in Kenya in accordance to chap. 486 of Company’s act. Started in 2009 as a sole proprietorship, Lazcom Solar has grown in customer base and service and it was then registered as a Limited Company in Kenya in 2013. We specialize in design, selection, purchase, servicing, maintenance, replacement, installation, testing and commissioning of all types of green energy solutions up to and including solar water heating system, power back up systems,Borehole drilling and Equiping, solar water pump installation and to carry on business as consultants, advisers and service providers in respect of
